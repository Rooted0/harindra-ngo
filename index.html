Harindra Samarpit Welfare Society

Empowering the disabled, elderly, widowed, and orphaned children through care, dignity, and sustainable support.

Our Mission

We are a dedicated welfare group committed to uplifting the most vulnerable members of our society. Our mission is to provide care, support, and resources to those who need it most.

Whether it's providing medical aid to the disabled, companionship to the elderly, support for widows, or education for orphaned children, we are here to lend a helping hand.

Our Causes

Disabled Support

Providing mobility aids, therapy, and inclusive opportunities for a dignified life.

Elderly Care

Ensuring our seniors live with respect, comfort, and the medical attention they deserve.

Widow Support

Empowering widows with vocational training, financial aid, and emotional support.

Orphan Care

Providing shelter, education, and a loving environment for children without parents.
